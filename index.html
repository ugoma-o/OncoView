What radiation is doing

Radiation uses targeted high-energy beams to treat tumor cells while limiting dose to nearby normal tissues whenever possible.

What the patient should expect

Treatment usually begins with consultation, simulation, immobilization, planning, quality assurance, then daily or near-daily treatment visits depending on the regimen.
